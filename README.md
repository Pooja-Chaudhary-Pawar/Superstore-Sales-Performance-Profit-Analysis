# Superstore-Sales-Performance-Profit-Analysis


## Project Overview

This project analyzes Superstore sales data to identify business trends, improve profitability, and support data-driven decision-making. The analysis was performed using Excel, Power Query, MySQL, Python, and Power BI to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights through an interactive dashboard.



##  Problem Statement

Businesses generate large volumes of sales data, making it difficult to manually identify sales trends, profitable products, customer purchasing behavior, and regional performance. This project aims to analyze historical sales data and build an interactive dashboard that helps management make informed business decisions.



##  Project Objectives

* Clean and transform raw sales data.
* Analyze monthly sales and revenue trends.
* Identify top-performing products and categories.
* Evaluate the impact of discounts on profitability.
* Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
* Compare regional sales and profit performance.
* Develop an interactive Power BI dashboard for business reporting.

##  Key Insights

* Monthly revenue trends help monitor business growth.
* Top-performing products contribute significantly to total revenue.
* Higher discounts can negatively affect profitability.
* Regional performance varies across different locations.
* Customer analysis helps identify high-value customers.



##  Business Recommendations

* Reduce excessive discounts on low-profit products.
* Focus marketing on high-performing products.
* Increase inventory for best-selling products.
* Develop customer retention strategies.
* Monitor KPIs regularly through interactive dashboards.
